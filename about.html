About Dark Thunder Games

Dark Thunder Games is a team that was made in April 2022

Dark Thunder Games makes games for people to play and enjoy

History of Dark Thunder Games

Dark Thunder Games was originally called Goofy Games Studios

Until late 2023, Goofy Games Studios mostly made games on Julians Editor and Scratch.

These games weren't very good, the scratch games was clicker games

and the Julians Editor games were mostly joke or short games.

On the 17th of June 2024, Goofy Games Studios was rebranded to Dark Thunder Games
